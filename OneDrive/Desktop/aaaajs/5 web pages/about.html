  1. A teacher, scientist and the people's President, APJ Abdul Kalam dedicated his entire life to hard work and public service.
  2. APJ Abdul Kalam, a man of simple taste, was keen to develop India. In order to inspire the people of the country, he authored around 25 books. He loved writing poems, one of which he dedicated to his mother.
  3. APJ Abdul Kalam always believed education enlightens human beings. None of his family members were educated, but he had a strong desire to learn and was persistent in making a difference.
  4. APJ Abdul Kalam grew up as a multi-religious individual, thanks to his famiy's spiritual simplicity. Born Muslim, he was a strong believer of secularism. He read Bhagavad Gita and Quran, and believed in God.
