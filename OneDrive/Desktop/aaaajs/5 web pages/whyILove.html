Why I love him

  • He was born into a family with limited means and started selling newspapers as a child to supplement his family's income
  • When his co-worker could not take his child for a carnival, Dr. Kalam himself accompanied the kid to the fair.
  • He was the first President to be sworn in without hailing from a political background. He was a scientist before and a teacher after his Presidency tenure
  • In the Rashtrapati Bhawan, he often had his meals on the floor along with the staff who worked with him.
  • He was the cleanest politician India had ever seen and remained uninvolved in political nuances even during his tenure.
